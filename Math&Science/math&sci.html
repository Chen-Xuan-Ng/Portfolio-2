  • eThink Challenge 2022

    I decided to present the Doppler Effect after researching on the internet for interesting science concepts. I found the Doppler Effect interesting as it had never occurred to me why the pitch of sound changes depending on how the object producing it moves. To present my topic, I made use of various resources such as science websites and videos to produce my own LEGO™ animation video on how the Doppler Effect works.

    Additional infomation on applications of the Doppler Effect: I find it fascinating that it is also used to guide military missiles to their targets.
